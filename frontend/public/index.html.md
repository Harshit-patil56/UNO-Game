## UNO No Mercy Rules & Features

**UNO Show 'Em No Mercy** (often called UNO No Mercy) is the brutally enhanced version of the classic UNO card game. Its two defining mechanics are:
1. **Draw Card Stacking**: Draw Two, Wild Draw Two, and Wild Draw Four penalties accumulate until someone cannot counter and absorbs the entire pile.
2. **Mercy Rule**: Instantly eliminates any player whose hand reaches 25 or more cards.

### Core Rules
- **Objective**: Be the first to empty your hand, or be the last player standing after others are eliminated.
- **Stacking Draw Cards**: Draw Two and Wild Draw Two stack. Counter with an equal or higher draw card to pass the penalty. Wild Draw Four stacks only with other Wild Draw Fours.
- **25-Card Mercy Rule**: Any player holding 25+ cards at the end of a turn is immediately eliminated.
- **UNO Call**: Declare UNO with 1 card remaining. Opponents can catch you — getting caught means drawing 2 penalty cards.
- **Wild Draw Four Challenge**: Only playable when you have no matching color. Challenger wins if bluff is confirmed (offender draws 4); failed challenge costs 6 cards.
- **Seven (7) Swap**: Swap hands with any player of your choice.
- **Zero (0) Pass**: Rotate all hands one position in the current play direction.

### Special Cards in No Mercy
- **Draw Two (+2)**: Next player draws 2 and skips, unless they counter-stack.
- **Wild Draw Two**: Choose color; next player draws 2. Stackable with Draw Two.
- **Wild Draw Four (+4)**: Choose color; next player draws 4. Challengeable. Stackable with Wild Draw Four only.
- **Wild Draw Six (+6)**: Choose color; next player draws 6.
- **Wild Draw Ten (+10)**: Choose color; next player draws 10.
- **Skip Everyone**: All other players skipped — you take an extra turn immediately.
- **Discard All**: Instantly discard every card of a chosen color from your hand.
- **Wild Shuffle Hands**: Collect all hands, shuffle, redistribute at random.
- **Reverse**: Reverses play direction.
- **Skip**: Skips the next player.
- **Wild**: Change active color.

## UNO Flip Rules & Features

UNO Flip features a double-sided deck. Play starts on the **Light Side** (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow). When a Flip card is played, all hands, the draw pile, and discard pile flip to the **Dark Side** (Orange, Pink, Teal, Purple), introducing new cards:
- **Draw Five (+5)**: Next player draws 5.
- **Skip Everyone**: Skips all other players.
- **Wild Draw Color**: Next player draws until they get the chosen color.
