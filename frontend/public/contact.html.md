# Contact Us - UNO With Friends

Have feedback, found a bug, or want to contribute? Use the contact form on the website to send a message directly to the developer!

---

## Contact Options
You can submit a message containing the following fields:
- **Name**: Your display name or handle.
- **Email**: Your email address so I can get back to you.
- **Message**: Your bug report, feedback, feature suggestion, or message.

---

## How to Get in Touch
Visit the [Contact Page](https://unomultiplayer.me/contact.html) directly to fill out the form. I appreciate all feedback and bug reports to help improve the Beta experience of UNO With Friends.
