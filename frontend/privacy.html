No Personal Data Collected

I value your privacy. UNO With Friends does not require user accounts, email registration, phone numbers, or passwords. Your selected display name is stored strictly in the temporary server memory for the duration of your active game session, and is deleted immediately when you disconnect from the room.

Local Storage Usage

I utilize standard browser Local Storage strictly to remember your preferences between visits. This includes saving your last entered player name, your avatar selection seed, and your sound preference (mute/unmute). This information stays on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

No Cookies or Advertising

UNO With Friends contains zero tracking cookies, marketing scripts, or third-party ads. I do not track your browsing history or sell user details to advertisers. The site is built solely for clean, ad-free multiplayer card gaming.
